Dhyanam

▾
suklambara-dharam vishnum sasi-varnam chaturbhujam |
prasanna-vadanam dhyayet sarva-vighnopa-santaye || 1 ||
yasya-dviradam-vaktradyah parishadyah paras satam |
vighnam nighnanti satatam visvaksenam tamasraye || 2 ||
vyasam vasistha-naptaram sakteh pautrama-kalmasam |
pararasatmajam vande sukatatatam taponidhim || 3 ||
vyasaya vishnu-rupaya vyasa-rupaya vishnave |
namo vai bramhanidhaye vasisthaya namo namah || 4 ||
avikaraya suddhaya nityaya paramatmane |
sadaika-rupa-rupaya vishnave sarvajishnave || 5 ||
yasya smarana-matrena janma-samsara-bandhanat |
vimuchyate namastasmai vishnave prabha-vishnave || 6 ||
Om namo vishnave prabha-vishnave |
Meditation Verses
Ksiro-danvat-pradese suci-mani vilasat saikate mauktikanam
malaklup-tasanastha sphatika-mani-nibhair mauktikair-manditangah |
subhrair-abhrai-radabhrai-rupari viracitair mukta-piyusha varshaih
anandi nah puniyadari-nalina-gada sankha-pani-r-mukundah || 1 ||
bhuh padau yasya nabhir-viyadasura-nilas-candra suryau cha netre
karnav-asas siro-dyau-r-mukhamapi dahano yasya vasteya-mabdhih |
antastham yasya visvam sura-nara-khaga-gobhogi-gandharva-daityaih
citram-ram ramyate tam tribhuvana vapusam vishnum-isam namami || 2 ||
Om namo bhagavate vasudevaya!
santakaram bhujaga-sayanam padmanabham suresam
visvadaram gagana-sadrsam megha-varnam subhangam |
laksmi-kantam kamala-nayanam yogi-hrd dhyana-gamyam
vande vishnum bhava-bhaya-haram sarva-lokaika-natham || 3 ||
megha-syamam pita-kauseya-vasam sriv-atsakam kaustudho-dbhasitangam |
punyopetam pundarikayataksham vishnum vande sarva-lokaika-natham || 4 ||
namas samasta bhutanam adi-bhutaya bhubhrte |
aneka-rupa rupaya vishnave prabha-vishnave || 5 ||
sa-sankha-cakram sa-kirita-kundalam sa-pita-vastram sarasi-ruheksanam |
sa-hara-vakhas-sthala-sobhi-kaustubham namami vishnum sirasa catur-bhujam || 6 ||
chayayam pari-jatasya hema-simha-sanopari
asinamambu-da-syama mayataksha malankrtam || 7 ||
chandra-nanam catur-bahum srivatsakita-vaksasam
rukmini satya-bhamabhyam sahitam krshna-masraye || 8 ||

Poorvangam

▾
Sri Vaisampyana Uvacha
srutva dharma-nasesena pavanani cha sarvasah |
yudhishthirah santanavam punareva-abhya-bhasata || 7 ||
Yudhishthira Uvacha
kimekam daivatam loke? kim va-apyekam parayanam?
stuvantah kam? kamarchantah prapnuyur-manavah subham? || 8 ||
ko dharmas sarva-dharmanam bhavatah paramo matah?
kim japan-muchyate jantur janma-samsara bandhanat? || 9 ||
Sri Bhishma Uvacha
jagat-prabhum deva-devam anantam purushottamam |
stuvannama sahasrena purushas satatotthitah || 10 ||
tameva carchayannnityam bhaktya purusha-mavyayam |
dhyayan stuvannamasyamsca yajamanas tameva cha || 11 ||
anadi nidhanam vishnum sarvaloka mahes-varam |
lokad-hyaksham stuvannityam sarva-duhkhatigo bhavet || 12 ||
brahmanyyam sarva-dharmajnam lokanam kirti-vardhanam |
lokanatham mahadbhutam sarva-bhuta bhavodbhavam || 13 ||
esha me sarva-dharmanam dharmo-adhika-tamomatah |
yadbhaktya pundarikaksham stavair-archennaras sada || 14 ||
paramam yo mahattejah paramam yo mahattapah |
paramam yo mahad-bramha paramam yah parayanam || 15 ||
pavitrannam pavitram yo mangalanam cha mangalam |
daivatam devatananam cha bhutanam yo-avyayah pita || 16 ||
yatas sarvani bhutani bhavantyadi yugagame |
yasmims-ca pralayam yanti punareva yugakshaye || 17 ||
tasya loka-pradhanasya jagannathasya bhupate |
vishnor-nama sahasram me srnu papa-bhayapaham || 18 ||
yani namani gaunani vikhyatani mahatmanah |
rshibhih parigitani tani vakshyami bhutaye || 19 ||
Viniyoga / Nyasah
rshir-namnam sahasrasya vedavyaso mahamunih |
chhando-anushthup tatha devo bhagavan devakisutah || 20 ||
amritam-su-udbhavo bijam saktirdevakinandanah |
trisama hrdayam tasya santyarthe viniyujyate || 21 ||
vishnum jishnum mahavishnum prabhavishnum mahesvaram |
anekarupa daityantam namami purushottamam || 22 ||
asya sri vishnor divya sahasranama stotra mahamantrasya |
sri vedavyaso bhagavan rshih | anushthup cchandah |
srimahavishnu-h parmatma srimannarayano devata |
amritamsu-udbhavo bhanuriti bijam | devaki-nandanas srashtheti saktih |
udbhavah kshobhano deva iti paramo-mantrah |
sankha-bhrn-nandaki chakri-ti kilakam | sarnga-dhanva gadadhara ity-astram |
rathangapani rakshobhya iti netram | trisama-samagas sameti kavacam |
anandam parabramheti yonih | rtu-ssudar-sanah kala iti digbandhah |
srivis-varupa iti dhyanam | sri mahavishnu prityarthe sahasranama jape viniyogah |
