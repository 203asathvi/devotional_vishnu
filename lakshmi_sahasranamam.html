Stotram — 1000 Names

▾
ॐ श्री महालक्ष्म्यै नमः — Om Sri Mahalakshmyai Namah
sarva-mangala-mangalye shive sarvartha-sadhike |
sharanye tryambake devi narayani namostu te || 154 || (chant 3 times)
punah punarnamaste'stu sashtangam ayutam punah |

Phala Srutih — Benefits

▾
Sanatkumara Uvacha
evam stuta maha-lakshmih brahma-rudradi-bhis-suraih |
namad-bhir-artadinaisca nissvatvair-bhoga-varjitaih || 1 ||
jyeshtha-jushtaisca nihsrikaih samsarat-svapa-rayanai |
vishnu-patni dadau tesham darsanam drishti-tarpanam || 2 ||
Mahalakshmi Uvacha
namnam sastha-sahasram me pramadad-vapi yah sakrit |
kirtayet tat-kule satyam vasamy-acandra-tarakam || 4 ||
kim punar niyamaj-japtor-mad-eka-sharanasya ca |
matruvat-sanukampaham poshaki syam aharni-sam || 5 ||
man-nama stavatamloke durlabham nasti cintitam |
mat-prasadena sarve-api svasveshtam avapsyatha || 6 ||
dadami sarvam ishtartham lakshmiti smaratam dhruvam |
Sanatkumara Uvacha
ityuktva-antardadhe lakshmih vaishnavi bhagavat-kala || 11 ||
tad-etat pravadamyadya lakshmi-nama-sahasrakam |
yoginah pathata kshipram cintitarthanavapsyatha || 13 ||
Occasion of Recitation: Ashtami, Chaturdasi, Navami, Fridays, Purnima, Amavasya, and all Parvakala (holy days). One who chants this daily with devotion attains all desired ends and Lakshmi's grace for generations.
|| Iti Sri Skanda Purane Sanatkumara Samhitayam Lakshmi Sahasranama Stotram Sampurnam ||
ॐ श्री महालक्ष्म्यै नमः
